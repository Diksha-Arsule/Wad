Training & Placement Cell - AVCOE

The Training & Placement Cell at AVCOE focuses on equipping students with the right skills and knowledge to excel in their careers. Through various programs, workshops, and industry collaborations, the cell ensures students are well-prepared for job opportunities.

Activities Organized

  • Value Added Courses
  • Soft Skills Development Programme
  • Expert Lectures/Workshops
  • Industrial Visits
  • Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp
  • Competitive Exam Cell
  • Industry-Institute Interaction
  • Sponsored Projects in the Industry
  • Expert Lectures and Workshops
  • In-Plant Training in the Industry
