Admissions at AVCOE

Join one of the leading engineering colleges in Maharashtra. Explore our undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs.

Eligibility & Admission Process

First Year B.E.: Candidates must have passed 12th (HSC) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics and appeared for MHT-CET or JEE.

ME & MBA Programs: Graduate in a relevant field with a valid GATE or CET score.

PhD: Postgraduate in respective engineering disciplines and must qualify for entrance as per university norms.

Courses Offered

  • B.E. in Computer Engineering
  • B.E. in Information Technology
  • B.E. in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.E. in Electrical Engineering
  • B.E. in Civil Engineering
  • MBA (Masters in Business Administration)
  • M.E. (Master of Engineering)
  • PhD in Mechanical & Civil Engineering
